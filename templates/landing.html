How it works

  1. In LINE, send a number (e.g. 1) to choose a folder, then send photos, videos, files or links.
  2. They are saved to Nextcloud under LINE_Backup / folder / date / type (image, video, link, files).
  3. Click "Open Nextcloud" to see your files; click "Manage source map" to edit number → folder (password required).
